Version 14.6

Asset Changes? No

Bug fixes

  • Fixed a bug/exploit where Tag Team Sigma could take the flag with a Maverick.
  • Fixed a bug/exploit where Zero could cancel the end lag of down thrust attacks.
  • Fixed a bug/exploit where Zero could use E-Blade and Ryuenjin mid-air.
  • Fixed a bug/exploit with the custom sprite setting where players could enter online matches with tampered sprite data.
  • Fixed a bug where Axl's level 3 copy shot only used 1 ammo instead of 8.

Balance changes

Mega Man X

  • Charged Magnet Mine: added flinch cooldown of 1 second.

Zero

  • Awakened Zero: Shingetsurin no longer costs scrap, but added flinch cooldown of 1 second.

Sigma

  • Added "Tough Guy" passive trait. This causes all flinches received to become mini-flinches, and total immunity to mini-flinches.
  • Puppeteer Mode: Increased Sigma base defense to 25% (75% when guarding).
  • Tag Team Mode: Maverick scrap cost reduced to 3.
  • Boomer Kuwanger: Can no longer teleport when Boomerang Cutter is being thrown.
  • Spark Mandrill: Increased Electric Spark cooldown from 1.25 to 2 seconds.
