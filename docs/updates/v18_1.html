Bug Fixes

  • Fixed an exploit where X could shoot uncharged X-buster at an unintended rate of fire with special buster.
  • Fixed a bug where Armored Armadillo roll could randomly get cancelled.
  • Fixed a bug with Hyper Charge being broken when used with Parasitic Bomb.

Balance Changes

  • Removed puppet cancel cooldown of 0.25s when switching to a Maverick.
  • Reduced Frog and Hawk ride armors to 24 HP without ability to gain HP from Heart Tanks.
  • Reduced Tag Team HP from 32 to 24 without ability to gain HP from Heart Tanks.
  • Reduced scrap cost of Tag Team from 6 to 5.
