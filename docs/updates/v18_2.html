Version 18.2

Asset Changes? Yes

Relay Server Update? No

Improvements

  • X2 mavericks can now be selected in 1v1.
  • Various sprite fixes and improvements.
  • Optimized shader code to reuse shaders and avoid recompiling the shader on every actor spawn.
  • Applied several shader fixes (X's palette and a few others) for older GPUs.
  • Added a graphics option for full screen rounding for low resolution monitors.

Bug Fixes

  • Fixed a bug/exploit where X could upgrade armor with special buster.
  • Fixed a Vulcan ammo exploit.
  • Fixed a bug where Zero could reflect projectiles from behind.
  • Fixed a visual bug with Flame Stag's antler in grab fall animation.
  • Fixed a bug where Flame Stag's grab fall had collision issues on some inclines.
  • Fixed water zone issues with Giant Dam and Forest2 maps.
  • Fixed a bug where attack hitboxes could collide with spikes, killing the player.
  • Fixed a bug where Fake Zero would stop dashing after moving a certain distance.
  • Fixed a bug where Viral sigma wouldn't stop moving if hit by knockback damage.
  • Fixed a bug where Magna Centipede could teleport into lava or spikes.
  • Fixed a bug where Magna Centipede Magnet Mine stopped damaging sometimes.
  • Fixed a bug where teleporting as Magna Centipede could mess up the camera.
  • Fixed a bug where Heart Tanks could add HP to Mavericks/Ride Armors in the current life.
  • Fixed a bug where certain Mavericks could latch onto moving walls.
  • Fixed a bug where damage text was too large in large camera.
  • Fixed a bug where Wolf Sigma could not revive next to Viral Sigma.
  • Fixed a bug where Vile could get stuck in ride armor menu if entering a neutral ride armor with Vile ride armor menu open.
  • Fixed a bug where Morph Moth cocoon size can not syncing.
  • Fixed a bug where Moth Moth Beam was not working in FTD.
  • Fixed extreme slowdown and crashes with Speed Devil shader on older machines.
  • Fixed a bug where Viral Sigma could move out of map boundaries when in attack states.

Balance Changes

General

  • Subtank cooldown reworked:
    • Cooldown time increased to 2 seconds.
    • Cooldown now only activates in combat (when dealing or receiving non-DoT damage) or after certain invuln frame moves end.
    • Removed locking in upgrade menu in use.
  • Healers (Silk Shot X, Morph Moth cocoon + moth) can no longer fill subtanks of heal target.
  • The following weapons can no longer assist:
    • Uncharged Spin Wheel
    • Uncharged Gravity Well
    • Uncharged Tornado Fang
    • Distance Needler

Mega Man X

  • X3 Chips can now earn scrap.
  • Hadouken/Shoryuken no longer rely on full health for use but now have a 30-second cooldown between uses.

Zero

Vile

  • Reworked Ride Armors:
    • Flinch replaced with pushback only which also reduces movement speed. (K has 50% reduced pushback; G does not have any at all.)
    • N/K/G armor speed and jump height reduced to v9.0 levels.
    • N/G armors now can hover again.
    • All armors except F had their stomp damage cooldown increased from 0.5s to 0.75s which will help reduce stomp loops with hover and H armor.
    • Note: N speed and hover changes do not apply to overworld ride armors.
  • No Ride Armors can cancel the enter animation anymore. (Previously all Rides except N could do so)

Axl

  • Blast Launcher mines can now be destroyed by enemies, and no longer detonate when destroyed.
  • Revolver Barrel damage reduced to 0.5 on bodyshot.
  • Ancient Gun no longer reflectable.

Sigma

  • Striker Mode no longer uses energy for most Mavericks (except Cocoon Moth to prevent excessive self healing).
  • Striker Mode made more usable with Mavericks like Wire Sponge by having attacks complete before they teleport out.
  • Tag Team: Mavericks can longer use Sub Tanks.
  • Chill Penguin: all attacks now share a cooldown.
  • Armored Armadillo: no longer loses armor at 50% health.
  • Armored Armadillo: guarding now slowly drains the ammo bar. If empty, guard cannot be used.
  • Wire Sponge: chain spinning damage reduced from 2 to 1.
  • Wheel Gator: wheels now slow down on hit, like with X's spin wheels. To compensate, a 1s flinch cooldown was added.
  • Bubble Crab: Bubble Splash now only launches 4 rings in one burst.
  • Bubble Crab: Reduced rate of damage of claws.
  • Bubble Crab: Crabling health reduced from 6 to 4 (2 for the bubble, 2 for the crab).
  • Flame Stag: fireball rate of fire reduced.
  • Flame Stag: grab damage reduced to 3 ceiling / 5 ground.
  • Flame Stag: speed burner cooldown now starts at end of the dash, not the charge.
  • Morph Moth: beam damage reduced to 5.
  • Morph Moth: beam now strong against Magna Centipede and Magnet Mine X.
  • Magna Centipede: Magnet Pull time now has a limit of 2 seconds and a cooldown of 2 seconds between uses.
  • Magna Centipede: Magnet Mine control time now has a limit of 4 seconds.
  • Magna Centipede: Magnet Mine damage reduced to 3.
  • Magna Centipede: added a 0.15s teleport cooldown (now consistent with Boomer Kuwanger).
  • Magna Centipede: teleport speed reduced from 300 to 200 units (same as run speed).
  • Overdrive Ostrich: damage on all 4 damage moves reduced to 3.
  • Overdrive Ostrich: now crystalized (but not damaged) when hit by charged c. hunter.
  • Overdrive Ostrich: max speed reduced from 350 units to 300 units.
  • Overdrive Ostrich: damage reduced to 2 and flinch reduced by half on running into foes.
  • Fake Zero: added a cooldown for green buster shots, but can now hold SHOOT for automatic fire.
  • Fake Zero: max speed reduced from 250 units to 200 units.
  • Fake Zero: ground sword damage at top speed reduced from 6 to 4 and mid speed from 4 to 3.
  • Fake Zero: shots are now reflectable.
  • Fake zero: 3rd hit of special combo (sword beam) damage reduced from 3 to 2.
  • Neo Sigma: second ground claw swing damage reduced to 2.
  • Neo Sigma: Cooldown added to successive up and down slashes.
  • Viral Sigma: Drill mechaniloid damage rate decreased.
  • Viral Sigma: no longer can possess flag carriers, or have possessed characters score or pick up flag.
  • Viral Sigma: Laser damage rate reduced.
  • Viral Sigma: Each successive possess increases time required +0.5 seconds, up to 4 seconds max. Also no longer easier to possess low HP enemies.
