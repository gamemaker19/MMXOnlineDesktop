Mega Man X4 Online: Deathmatch (Demo/Proof of Concept)

Download: Windows Download

Description: Mega Man X4 Online: Deathmatch demo! This was a work-in-progress around sometime around summer 2021. I had originally planned to end development on MMXOD after the X3 update and move onto MMX4OD, but I had a change in heart and dropped the project due to the continued playerbase of MMX1OD. It's unlikely I will actually finish this project. There is no online yet, or any X4 special weapons, and most of the maps are unfinished. NOTE: don't try loading the sprites/map JSONs from this game in the current MMXOD editor. They are outdated at this point and likely won't work, or if they still do, could result in glitchy behavior.
